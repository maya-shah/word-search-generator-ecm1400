

Welcome to my word search generator game!
Follow the instructions below to start playing :)

----------------------- How to play -----------------------

- You will be asked to guess a word from the word search board or if you've
entered 'FINISHED'.

- If you would like to stop the game at any point enter 'FINISH'.

- You will score points for any word entered within 60 seconds of the game
starting.

- The points are calculated based on the combined length of all the words
you have found.

Good Luck!
